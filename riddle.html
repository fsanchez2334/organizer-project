Brain Teaser Section

Brain Teaser #1: Puzzle at the Morgue

A family of four has been asked to go to the morgue to identify a body who may be their daughter who went missing a month before. Upon arriving the family doesn't recognize the body, only the mortician, who identifies himself as a lost son. When asked about his name, he states "Janus", prompting the father to say: "Our daughter is not dead, for she was never born" How is this possible?

Brain Teaser #2: Mystery Through a Mirror

A young girl once entered a hallway filled with mirrors, to which there is a door being reflected. However, the door is only being reflected through 7 of the 14 mirrors, her human being reflected over through five other mirrors. What other object is being reflected over the other two mirrors?

Brain Teaser #3: A Life in Four Cards

While playing with a gambler, the player is told that he can win if he can find the king of diamonds in a group of 5 cards dealt upside down. He flips a card and sees that it is the Ace of Spades. The player states: The third one has the king; he wins without getting the king of of diamonds. How?

Brain Teaser #4: Question at the Round Table

Four people are sitting around a round table, and each has a different profession. The goal is for a person to be the first one to deduce who works as a teacher; however, only one hint can be provided for everyone at the table. Each of them say I work at the front desk I run while at work I don't work in the presence of light We all work at a school Who is the teacher?
